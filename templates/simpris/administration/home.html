Information for Administrators

Each Simpris account has at least one administrator, including the person who created the account.

Simpris administrators create projects and assign users to them. To ensure security Simpris only allows new projects to be edited by administrators until users have been added.

It is best not to create too many administrators - standard users can do everything they need to be able to work on tasks and problems.
