Welcome to Simpris

To get the most out of Simpris we advise that you consult the help pages listed on the right. At the least you will need to understand the basic concepts on which the system depends.

However Simpris is very powerful beneath the surface without being complex to use, so you should be very productive very soon!

After you have read the help sections to the left, perhaps you can start by creating your first client or project.

If you have work with client companies or organisations, start by creating new organisations: to do this hover your mouse over 'My clients' and click the 'Add client' menu option. Add a new client organisation.

Added your client organisation? You can have as many clients as you wish. Now add a new project. To do this hover over 'People & Clients' above, then click 'Add project'. Enter details of your project.

Added your project? Now you can add some users for your project. Hover your mouse over 'People & Clients' and click on 'Add people'. Add as many people as you need - all the people you will be working with: employees of the clients you have created, co-workers, freelancers, partners and so forth.

Added your colleagues? Now go back to your project and give access to those who need it. Remember not all of your colleagues will need access to every project. It is good practice to only give people access to the projects they need to work on, especially if they are outside freelancers or contractors.

Now add some tasklists to your project - this enables you to break the project up into manageable pieces.

Now add some tasks to your tasklists. Then watch as your colleagues administer the projects for you leaving you to manage and direct.

After a while you may decide that you like Simpris and wish to pay for a subscription. To see the advantages this gives, take a look at our upgrade page.
